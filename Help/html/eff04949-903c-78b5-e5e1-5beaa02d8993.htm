Remarks
This method works around the ambiguity reported when comparing a UInt64 operand with a signed operand. If the UInt64 operand is greater than MaxValue, it is automatically known to be greater than the signed operand; otherwise, it is safely cast to the type Int64 and compared natively to the signed operand.
